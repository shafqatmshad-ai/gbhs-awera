Principal’s Message

It gives me great pleasure to welcome you to Government Boys High School Awera, Bagh. Since its establishment in 1952, our school has been committed to providing quality education and building a strong foundation for the future of our students. Education is not just about acquiring knowledge. it is about developing character, discipline, and a sense of responsibility. Our goal is to nurture young minds so they become confident, competent, and compassionate citizens who can contribute positively to society. With the dedicated efforts of our teachers and the continued support of parents and the community, our students consistently achieve excellent results in both the Elementary Board and Mirpur Board examinations. I am proud of our school’s achievements and remain confident that, together, we will continue to move forward with passion and purpose. — Principal Government Boys High School Awera, Bagh
