Overview

About Government Boys High School Awera Bagh

Government Boys High School Awera was established in 1952 with the aim of providing quality education to the youth of the region. Over the decades, the school has earned a reputation for excellence in both academics and co-curricular activities. Currently, the school has around 350 students enrolled across various classes. Each year, our students achieve remarkable results in the Elementary Board and Mirpur Board examinations, bringing pride and recognition to the institution. The school is dedicated to nurturing discipline, knowledge, and character among its students. With a team of committed teachers and a focus on continuous improvement, Government Boys High School Awera continues to play a vital role in shaping the future of our community.

School Facilities and Staff

Our school has a total staff of 19 members, including 1 Principal, 4 SSTs (Secondary School Teachers), 10 ESTs (Elementary School Teachers), 1 Clerk, 2 Naib Qasids, and 1 Chowkidar. The school building is well equipped with a computer lab, science lab, library, ECE room, and first aid kit. There are 7 washrooms available for students and staff. The school also has a spacious playground that provides students with opportunities for sports and physical activities.
